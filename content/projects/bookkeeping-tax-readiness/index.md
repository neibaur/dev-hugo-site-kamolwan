Practical finance operations case study focused on keeping small business records organized, current, and ready for review.

<!--more-->

The project is scoped around day-to-day accounting discipline: capturing sales and expense records, monitoring inventory and cost activity, reconciling payment records, and preparing documentation that can support tax preparation and financial reporting.

Current focus areas:

- Sales, expense, and receipt organization
- Inventory, vendor, and customer record tracking
- Account reconciliation and discrepancy review
- Tax preparation documentation and year-end readiness
